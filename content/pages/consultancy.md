# Consultancy Services

As a fully registered medical practitioner and experienced developer in Malaysia, I offer specialized consultancy services that bridge clinical expertise with digital innovation.

My practice focuses on three core areas: leading health tech projects from concept to deployment, creating clear and accurate healthcare communications, and providing strategic guidance that connects clinical insights with market realities. I work with healthcare organizations and technology partners to deliver solutions that are clinically sound, technically robust, and ready for real-world implementation.

## End-to-End Product & Project Management

I provide comprehensive leadership for health tech initiatives, guiding projects from initial concept to final deployment. My approach integrates clinical insights with technical project management, ensuring that solutions are not only delivered on time and within budget but are also clinically relevant, compliant, and ready for real-world adoption. I manage the entire lifecycle, from defining requirements to coordinating development teams and overseeing launch strategies.
