# Training Services

With expertise spanning healthcare technology, digital innovation, and clinical practice, I bring a solutions-oriented approach to education and training that prioritizes practical skills, evidence-based methods, and real-world application. Whether you need clinical upskilling, mental health education, or AI adoption training, I am committed to delivering professional, high-quality training services that meet rigorous medical and technical standards.

As a fully registered medical practitioner and experienced healthcare technology professional in Malaysia, I offer the following training services to healthcare organizations, educational institutions, and professional bodies.

---

## Clinical & Acute Care Training

I work together with NGOs and professional bodies, to develop modules and deliver specialized workshops on core medical topics, including metabolic health, primary care best practices, and Basic Life Support (BLS) certification. These practical sessions are designed to upskill participants, ensuring they are equipped with essential clinical knowledge and emergency response capabilities.

---

## Mental Health, Resilience & Crisis Response Education

I facilitate interactive sessions designed to improve organizational well-being. These workshops cover Psychological First Aid (PFA) for emergency situations, practical stress management techniques, and strategies for building emotional resilience and effective coping mechanisms in high-pressure environments.

---

## AI & HealthTech Adoption

I help patients, healthcare providers, clinics, and industry organizations successfully adopt and integrate AI and health technology solutions. My services include training end-users on digital health platforms, guiding clinical teams through AI-assisted workflows, supporting organizational change management for technology implementation, and consulting with industry partners on practical deployment strategies. I bridge the gap between cutting-edge health technology and real-world clinical practice, ensuring seamless adoption that improves patient outcomes, enhances provider efficiency, and drives innovation across the healthcare ecosystem.
