# AI Workflow Consulting

Your team has processes that work. But "works" and "works well" are different things, and the gap between them is where generative AI lives right now.

## The Problem

Most organizations know they should be using AI tools. Few know *where* to start, and fewer still know how to evaluate whether the tools are actually helping or just adding complexity.

## What We Offer

**Workflow Review & Mapping**: We sit with your team, observe how work flows, and map the process end-to-end. No assumptions, no templates. Your workflow, as it actually is.

**AI Opportunity Identification**: For each step in the workflow, we assess whether a generative AI tool (Claude, GPT, Gemini, or domain-specific models) can reduce time, reduce errors, or unlock capacity. We are model-agnostic and vendor-neutral.

**Implementation Roadmap**: We deliver a prioritized list of interventions, ranked by impact and ease of implementation. Quick wins first, structural changes later.

**Hands-on Setup**: If you want, we don't just advise. We build. We can set up the tools, configure the prompts, train your team, and monitor the first cycle.

## Who This Is For

This service is designed for small-to-medium organizations that want to adopt AI practically, not performatively. If you are looking for a slide deck about "digital transformation", we are not the right fit. If you want someone who will sit in your office, understand your pain points, and deliver working solutions, let's talk.

## Engagement Model

We offer this as a fixed-scope engagement: typically 2-4 weeks from initial briefing to delivered roadmap. Implementation support is available as a separate engagement.
