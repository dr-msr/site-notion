# Digital Infrastructure & Automation

Good infrastructure is invisible. You notice it only when it breaks. Our job is to make sure it doesn't.

## What We Build

**Cloud Architecture**: Design and deployment on modern cloud platforms. We work with Cloudflare, AWS, and GCP depending on what fits your use case and budget.

**API Integrations**: Connecting your tools so data flows where it needs to go, without manual intervention. CRM to billing, scheduling to notifications, intake forms to databases.

**Automation Pipelines**: Repetitive tasks that eat your team's time can usually be automated. We build pipelines that handle the routine so your people can handle the exceptions.

**DevOps & CI/CD**: For teams building software, we set up the infrastructure that keeps deployments smooth, tests running, and environments consistent.

## Philosophy

We prefer boring technology. Proven tools, simple architectures, clear documentation. The goal is a system your team can maintain without us. We build ourselves out of a job, not into one.

## Tech Stack

We are comfortable across a wide range of tools but tend toward: Docker, Cloudflare Workers, Python, Node.js, PostgreSQL, and Linux. We choose tools based on the problem, not on what's trending.
