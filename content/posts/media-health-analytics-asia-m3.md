# Health Analytics Asia: M3 Co-Founders Profile

Health Analytics Asia published a profile piece on the co-founders of Medical Mythbusters Malaysia (M3), focusing on the intersection of healthcare communication and digital reach in Southeast Asia. The piece examined how a group of practicing doctors leveraged social media to build a health literacy platform that reached over a million readers.

The profile explored the strategic thinking behind M3's approach: why doctors chose to engage directly with public misinformation rather than delegating to communications teams, and how the platform's credibility was built through consistent, evidence-based responses to viral health claims circulating in Malaysian social media.

Being featured in a regional health analytics publication signaled that M3's model was being noticed not just as a local initiative, but as a case study in digital health communication with relevance across the region.

Regional recognition of health communication work at the intersection of medicine and technology. Credibility as a source for industry-level analysis of digital health initiatives in Southeast Asia.
