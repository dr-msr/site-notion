# Malaysiakini — Medical Myths Debunking Coverage

Malaysiakini, Malaysia's leading independent online news portal, covered the medical myths debunking work being carried out through the Medical Mythbusters Malaysia initiative. The piece focused on specific examples of health misinformation circulating in Malaysian social media and the systematic approach developed to counter them.

Rather than treating health misinformation as isolated incidents, the coverage highlighted how the debunking work operated as a structured, repeatable process: identifying trending health claims, tracing them to their sources, evaluating them against evidence, and publishing accessible counter-narratives that could compete with the original misinformation for reach and engagement.

Malaysiakini's coverage was significant because of the outlet's reputation for rigorous, independent journalism. Being sourced by Malaysiakini on health misinformation signaled credibility in a space where trust is the most valuable currency.
